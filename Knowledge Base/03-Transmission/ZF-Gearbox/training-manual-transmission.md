## ZF Gearbox Overview

The Santana PS10 is equipped with a ZF manual transmission. This document provides training information on the ZF gearbox system.

### Gearbox Maintenance Schedule

From the maintenance manual, the following service intervals apply:

| Maintenance Item | Interval (KM) | Interval (Miles) | Interval (Months) | Condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Gear Box Oil | 30,000 | 18,000 | 24 | Replace or Change (R) |

### Oil Specifications

Refer to the transmission fluid specification guide provided in the dealer service materials. The gearbox requires specialized transmission fluid to maintain proper function and longevity.

### Key Service Points

1. **Oil Change Interval** - Every 30,000 km or 24 months
2. **Fluid Type** - Consult OEM specification for exact grade
3. **Fluid Capacity** - Check service manual for precise capacity
4. **Drain and Fill Procedure** - Follow detailed procedure in full service manual
