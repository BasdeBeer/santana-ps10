# Santana PS10 EIII - Transfer Case Training Manual

**Vehicle:** Santana PS10 EIII
**Engine:** Iveco 8140.43P (2.8 TD, Euro III)
**Source:** Dealer Training Manual - Transfer Case Section

---

## Transfer Case Overview

The Santana PS10 features a transfer case system for all-wheel drive capability. This document provides training information on the transfer case.

### Transfer Case Maintenance Schedule

From the maintenance manual, the following service intervals apply:

| Maintenance Item | Interval (KM) | Interval (Miles) | Interval (Months) | Operation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Transfer Box Oil | 30,000 | 18,000 | 24 | Replace or Change (R) |

### Service Operations

**Operation Code Meanings:**
- **R** - Replace or Change oil
- **I** - Inspect, correct, lubricate, clean as necessary

### Oil Specification

The transfer case requires specialized all-wheel drive transmission fluid. The specific type and capacity should be verified in the OEM service documentation.

---

## Key Service Areas

1. **Fluid Service** - Oil change every 30,000 km
2. **Fluid Type** - Check OEM specification for exact grade
3. **Fluid Capacity** - Confirm in complete service manual
4. **Drain/Fill Plugs** - Locate in service documentation
5. **Seal Inspection** - Visual check for leaks

---

## Torque Specifications

Transfer case fasteners require specific torque values. These will be detailed in the complete service manual section covering transfer case removal and installation.

---

## Important Maintenance Notes

- Change transfer case fluid at regular intervals to maintain proper operation
- Inspect for oil leaks at each service
- Replace fluid and filter if contaminated
- Do not overfill - follow capacity specifications
- Use only OEM-approved transmission fluid
