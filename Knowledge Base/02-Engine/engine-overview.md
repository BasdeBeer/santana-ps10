## Engine Identification and General Specifications

### Basic Engine Information

The Iveco 8140.43P is a 4-cylinder in-line diesel engine featuring a turbocharger with intercooler. This engine is used in the Santana PS10 and complies with Euro III emissions standards.

**Engine Variants:**
- **8140.43C.3** - Mechanical Injection variant with rotary pump
- **8140.43S.4** - Common Rail variant with EDC electronic control

### Engine Layout and Configuration

**Type:** 4-cylinder, in-line, water-cooled, turbocharged diesel

**Key Dimensions:**
- **Bore:** 94.4 mm
- **Stroke:** 100 mm
- **Displacement:** 2,800 cc (2.8 liters)
- **Compression Ratio:** 18.5:1

### Performance Specifications

**Power and Torque:**
- Power output and torque specifications vary by variant (mechanical injection vs. Common Rail)
- Engine management systems ensure optimal performance across all operating conditions

### Engine Cooling System

The cooling system uses forced circulation with an electromagnetic pulley clutch for fan control.

**Coolant Specifications:**
- **System Type:** Forced circulation with water pump
- **Thermostat Opening Range:** 79°C ± 2°C to 82°C ± 2°C
- **Maximum Operating Temperature:** 110°C
- **Capacity:** To be verified from cooling system section

**Electromagnetic Pulley Clutch:**
- Provides variable fan engagement based on thermal demands
- Reduces fuel consumption and noise when full cooling capacity is not required

### Fuel System Overview

Two injection system variants are offered:

**1. Mechanical Injection System (8140.43C.3)**
- **Type:** Rotary injection pump
- **Fuel Pump:** High-pressure mechanical pump
- **System Pressure:** Approximately 140 bar
- **Injection Control:** Mechanical governor

**2. Common Rail System (8140.43S.4)**
- **Type:** Common Rail fuel injection
- **Fuel Pressure:** Up to 1600 bar
- **Electronic Control:** EDC (Electronic Diesel Control)
- **Injectors:** Solenoid-operated direct injectors

### Emissions Control (Euro III)

The engine meets Euro III standards with the following features:

**Exhaust Aftertreatment:**
- Turbocharger with intercooler for improved air density
- Engine management systems optimized for low emissions
- Compliance with particulate matter and NOx limits
