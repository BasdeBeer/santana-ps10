## ZF Transmission System

The ZF gearbox (Zahn Fahr/Zahnradfabrik Friedrichshafen AG) is a German transmission manufacturer known for high-quality automatic and manual transmissions used in many commercial vehicles.

### Transmission Types Available:
1. **ZF Automatic Transmission** (if equipped)
   - Torque converter
   - Multiple-speed planetary gearsets
   - Electronic control modules

2. **ZF Manual Transmission** (if equipped)
   - Manual gear selection
   - Mechanical linkage
   - Clutch system

---

## Typical ZF System Components

### Transmission Housing & Case
- **Gearbox casting:** Main transmission body
- **Bellhousing:** Clutch interface
- **Sump:** Oil pan and drain plug
- **Filter:** Transmission fluid strainer
- **Seals:** Input and output shaft seals

### Internal Components
- **Gears:** Multiple speed gears
- **Bearings:** Shaft bearings
- **Syncros:** Synchronizer rings (manual trans)
- **Forks:** Shift forks
- **Shafts:** Input, output, and intermediate shafts

### External Controls
- **Shift linkage:** Manual shift cables
- **Brackets:** Transmission mounts
- **Bellhousing bolts:** Fastening hardware
- **Fluid lines:** ATF supply (automatic only)

### Electrical Components (Automatic)
- **Control module:** Transmission ECU
- **Solenoids:** Shift control solenoids
- **Sensors:** Speed and pressure sensors
- **Connectors:** Wiring harnesses

## ZF Gearbox Selection

The exact ZF transmission model depends on:
- Engine type (Type 1 vs Type 2)
- Vehicle configuration (wagon vs pickup)
- Market region
- Customer specification

Common ZF Models for Santana PS-10:
- **ZF 5HP** series (5-speed automatic)
- **ZF 6HP** series (6-speed automatic)
- **ZF Manual** series (4 or 5-speed manual)

## Fluid Specifications

**ATF (Automatic Transmission Fluid):**
- ZF TE-ML type
- Viscosity grade per ZF specification
- Capacity: Typical range 6-8 liters (check specific model)

**Gear Oil (Manual Transmission):**
- ZF LS type hypoid oil
- API GL-5 equivalent
- Capacity: Typical range 1.5-2.0 liters

---

## Maintenance Intervals

**Automatic Transmission:**
- Fluid change: 50,000-100,000 km (as per manual)
- Filter change: At each fluid change
- Fluid level check: Every service

**Manual Transmission:**
- Fluid level check: Every service
- Fluid change: 100,000+ km (as needed)
- No filter service required
