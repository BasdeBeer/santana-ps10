### Categories Covered:

1. **Lighting System**
   - Front lamps (headlights, parking lights)
   - Rear lamps (stop, reverse, fog)
   - Interior dome/room lights
   - License plate illumination

2. **Wiring & Connectors**
   - Main harnesses (main & floor variants)
   - Type 1 vs Type 2 variants
   - Room lamp circuits
   - Door switches

3. **Electrical Controls**
   - Relay modules
   - Fuse boxes
   - Electronic control units
   - Sensors and switches

4. **Charging System**
   - Battery assemblies
   - Alternator/Generator
   - Regulator

5. **Instruments**
   - Speedometer assembly
   - Electrical gauges
   - Warning lights

## Type Variants

**TYPE 1:** Mechanical fuel injection
- Different wiring harness routing
- Simpler control system
- Manual transmission variants

**TYPE 2:** Common rail diesel
- Enhanced wiring for electronic controls
- Additional sensors and connectors
- More complex harness configuration
