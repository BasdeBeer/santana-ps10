## System Overview

### Front Suspension Type
- Independent double wishbone or MacPherson strut (depending on variant)
- Coil springs with telescopic shock absorbers
- Stabilizer bar for roll control

### Rear Suspension Type
- Rigid axle with leaf springs or coil springs
- Trailing arms or semi-elliptic springs
- Shock absorbers

### Steering System
- Manual or power assisted (depending on variant and market)
- Recirculating ball or rack-and-pinion type
- Column-mounted or floor-mounted transmission

### Brake System
- Front disc brakes (ventilated)
- Rear drum brakes (standard) or disc brakes (option)
- Hydraulic servo assistance
- Mechanical parking brake

## Variants by Market

**Type 1 (Mechanical Injection):**
- Manual power steering (some markets)
- Standard brake configuration
- Basic suspension setup

**Type 2 (Common Rail):**
- Power steering with electronic control (some markets)
- Enhanced brake systems (ABS optional)
- Adjustable suspension (market dependent)
