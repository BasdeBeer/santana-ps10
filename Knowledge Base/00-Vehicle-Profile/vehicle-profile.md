# Santana PS10 - Vehicle Profile

## Identity
- **Make:** Santana Motor S.A. (Linares, Spain)
- **Model:** PS-10 (Station Wagon)
- **Year:** 2006 (approximately)
- **Variant:** Updated model with ZF gearbox, Euro III emissions
- **Body Numbers:** VSFPSW10V00100001~ / VSFPSW20V00100009~
- **Type:** Type 1 / Type 2 station wagon
- **Registration (NL):** 24-BX-HS

## Engine
- **Motor Code:** 8140.43P (Iveco)
- **Type:** 2.8 Turbo Diesel (2.8 TD), direct injection
- **Emissions Standard:** Euro III (EIII)
- **ECU/Injection:** EDC (Electronic Diesel Control) with blink-code diagnostics
- **Recommended Oil:** Shell Super Plus 10W/40 API SJ/CF
- **Oil Change Interval:** Every 30,000 km or 24 months (normal conditions)
  - For short yearly mileage or < 30,000 km: change every 12 months

## Transmission
- **Gearbox:** ZF manual transmission (updated spec, replacing earlier gearbox)
- **Transfer Case:** Part-time 4WD with transfer box
- **Differentials:** Front and rear

## Current Status
- **Odometer:** ~120,000 km
- **Note:** At 120,000 km the vehicle has completed a full maintenance cycle per the training manual schedule (which covers 15k-120k km intervals)

## Key Maintenance Milestones at 120,000 km
Per the training manual schedule, the following should have been done or are due:
- Timing belt: Should have been replaced at 90,000 km
- Accessory drive belt: Replace at 90,000 km
- Engine oil + filter: Replace (R)
- Air filter: Replace (R)
- Fuel filter: Replace (R)
- Fuel prefilter (common rail): Replace (R)
- Engine coolant: Replace (R)
- Brake fluid: Replace (R)
- Servo-steering hydraulic oil filter: Replace (R)
- Brake discs and blocks: Inspect (I)
- All transmission oils: Inspect (I)
- Clutch liquid/pedal: Inspect (I)
