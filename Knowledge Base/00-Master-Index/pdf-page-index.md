## Notes for Users

1. **Service Manual Sections:** Pages are labeled with section codes (e.g., OB for Maintenance and Lubrication). Use the index on the inside front cover to locate sections.

2. **Training Manual:** Designed for dealer technician training. Contains more detailed repair procedures than the Service Manual and includes torque specifications and adjustment procedures.

3. **Owner's Manual:** For driver-level information. Pages are numbered individually within sections (e.g., 1.1, 2.2). Use the table of contents on page 1 for navigation.

4. **Parts Catalogue:**
   - First check pages 3-43 (Updated 1st REM) for current parts
   - If not found, refer to pages 44+ (Original 1st Version)
   - All figures are cross-referenced in the Information Illustration Index

5. **Cross-References:** Most sections in the Service Manual reference detailed procedures in other sections. Follow these references for complete procedures.

6. **ZF Transmission Note:** This PS10 is equipped with the ZF LT-85 manual transmission. Transmission-specific information is found in:
   - Service Manual Section 7A
   - Training Manual Page 6
   - Parts Catalogue Section pages 6 and 273 (ZF-specific figures)

7. **Engine Specifications:** This PS10 features the Iveco 8140.43P engine (Euro III with EDC). Specifications are found in:
   - Service Manual Section 6-2
   - Training Manual Pages 1-16
   - Owner's Manual Page 11
   - Parts Catalogue Page 2, FIG. 40 (engine label)

8. **Electrical System Variants:** The PS10 may be equipped with electronic injection (EDC) or mechanical injection. Fuel system procedures vary:
   - Mechanical injection: Service Manual OB-13 (Mechanical section)
   - Common rail: Service Manual OB-14 (Common Rail section)
