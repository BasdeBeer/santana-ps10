# Vehicle Cleanliness and Care

## Keeping Your Vehicle Clean and Protected

7. VEHICLE CLEANING AND CARE
CORROSION PREVENTION
It is important to look after your vehicle
adequately to protect it from corrosion. In
the following information you will find
instructions on how to avoid corrosion.

Common corrosion causes
1.-Salt, dirt or humidity accumulation in
inaccessible places of the bottom part
of the body..
2.-Dents, scratches and any other damage
on metallic or painted surfaces.

Environmental conditions that
accelerate corrosion
1.-High salt concentration at seaside
zones.
2.-High humidity when temperature is
above freezing point.
3.-High temperatures accelerate corrosion
in those parts of the vehicle which are
not sufficiently ventilated.
4.-Humidity presence in parts of the vehicle
for a long period of time.
This information shows us the importance
of keeping your vehicle ( specially the
under part of the body), as clean and as
dry as possible, it is also important to repair
paint damage quickly.

7-3

How to help avoid corrosion
Wash your vehicle frequently.
The best way to keep your vehicle and
avoid corrosion is by keeping it clean
frequently. If your vehicle is used under
severe driving conditions then the washing
frequency should increase.
Eliminate strange object deposits.
Eliminate the deposits as soon as possible,
they could damage the finishing of your
vehicle. If they are difficult to eliminate, you
could use some sort of additional cleaner.

Use mud shields and gravel guards.
The use of mud shields and gravel guards
will help protect your vehicle, above all if
you usually travel on saline and gravel
tracks. The iron fittings must be anticorrosive.
Keep your vehicle in a dry ventilated
place.
Do not park your vehicle in wet and badly
ventilated places. Do not leave it wet in a
hot garage when ventilation is insufficient.

Damage to vehicle finishing
If you discover scratches or other damage
to the paintwork, repair it immediately to
prevent from corrosion. If the scratches
leave the metallic part visible, get a
trustworthy workshop to repair it.
Keep passenger and loading
compartments clean.
Humidity and dirt under the rush mats
could cause corrosion. Every now and
then, check under the mats to check for
cleanliness and dryness.
If you carry corrosive substances, do so
in sealed containers.

## Maintenance Benefits

Regular cleaning and care:
- Protects paint and body from corrosion
- Preserves interior trim and upholstery
- Maintains visibility through windows
- Extends overall vehicle life
