## Starting the Engine

Follow proper engine starting procedures. Allow the engine to warm up before driving, especially in cold weather.

## Regular Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential for:
- Optimal vehicle performance and reliability
- Extended vehicle service life
- Safety of occupants
- Maintenance of warranty coverage
