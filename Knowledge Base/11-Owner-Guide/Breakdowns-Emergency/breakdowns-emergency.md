## What to Do in a Breakdown

1. Move vehicle to a safe location away from traffic
2. Turn on hazard lights
3. Place warning triangles if available
4. Contact roadside assistance
5. Do not attempt unsafe repairs

Your vehicle is equipped with front and rear towing hooks for emergency towing recovery.
