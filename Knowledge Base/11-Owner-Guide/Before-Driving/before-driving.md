## Key Points Before Driving

- Ensure all doors are locked while driving
- Adjust driver's seat and mirrors for optimal visibility
- Fasten safety belts properly before starting engine
- Check that the engine bonnet is completely secured
- Verify all windows and doors are functioning correctly
