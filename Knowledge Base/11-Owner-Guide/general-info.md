ENGINE SERIES NUMBER

The chassis series number is is tamped
on the right front part of the chassis, as
shown in the figure. Chassis series
numbers are used for vehicle registration.
They are also used for some oredrs or
special servicing information. Whenever
consulting a Santana distributor , identify
your vehicle with those numbers. If it is
difficult to identify those numbers, you may
also find them in the Technical Filing Card
of your vehicle.

The engine series number, is stamped on
the right side as can be seen in the figure.
