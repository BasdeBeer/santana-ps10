# Towing Rules

## Towing Your Vehicle

9. TOWING AND DRAGGING RULES
Rear wheels lifted
The vehicle can be towed by a towing truck
with rear wheels lifted and the front wheels
on the ground. The transporting speed
must not exceed 50 Km/h (30 Mph).
For this tow type:
1.- Adjust the manual free wheels to
"FREE" position (if it is equipped
with automatic free wheels, free
them).
2.- If your vehicle is not equipped with
any type of free wheel, drag the
vehicle on a sliding ramp under the
front wheels. If you do not have a
sliding ramp, transport it on its front
wheels on the ground in the
following conditions:
a) Gear stick in neutral position.
b) Transfer lever in "2H".
c) The distance to do is less than 160 Km
(96 M).
3.- Turn the starting key to "1" position to
free the steering wheel.
4.- Check that front wheels are aligned
forwardly and tie the steering wheel with
specialy designed apparel for towing
services.

## Towing Equipment

Your Santana PS10 features:
- Front towing hooks for recovery
- Rear towing hooks for emergency towing

## Towing Safety

- Verify local regulations before towing
- Use appropriate towing equipment
- Follow speed limits for towing
- Secure load properly
- Use safety chains when required
