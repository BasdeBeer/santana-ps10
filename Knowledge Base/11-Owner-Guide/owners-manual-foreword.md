## Important Safety Information

This manual contains essential information about the safe operation and maintenance of your Santana PS10. Key points to remember:

- **WARNING**: Indicates potential danger which could cause death or bodily injuries
- **CAUTION**: Indicates potential danger which could cause vehicle damage
- **NOTE**: Special information to facilitate vehicle maintenance or clear up important instructions

Keep this manual in your vehicle for reference.
