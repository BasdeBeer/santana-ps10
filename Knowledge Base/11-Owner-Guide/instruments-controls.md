## Warning Lights Reference

Your PS10 is equipped with several warning lights and indicators:

**Critical Warnings (Red)**
- Oil Pressure: Low oil pressure - stop immediately
- Brake System: Brake failure or low fluid
- Engine Temperature: Overheating
- Battery Charge: Charging system failure

**Indicator Lights (Green/Blue)**
- Turn Signal: Directional indicators
- High Beam: High-beam headlights on
- 4WD Mode: Four-wheel drive engaged
- Parking Brake: Hand brake applied
