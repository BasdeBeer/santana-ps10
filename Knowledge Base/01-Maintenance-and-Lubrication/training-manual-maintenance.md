## Severe Driving Condition Codes

- **A** - Short and frequent trips
- **B** - Driving through muddy paths and/or grounds
- **C** - Driving through paths and/or dusty grounds
- **D** - Driving on very cold weather and/or salty paths
- **E** - Short and frequent trips under cold weather
- **G** - Town driving / towing / high speed driving / Temperatures above 40°C / Lubricants and fuel of low quality
- **H** - Towing
- **I** - Hired Vehicles

---

## Maintenance Operation Codes

| Code | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **R** | Replace or change |
| **I** | Inspect, correct, lubricate, clean, purge or tighten, if necessary |
| **\*1** | INSPECT OR REPLACE MORE OFTEN IF NECESSARY |

---

## Notes

- The maintenance schedule provided covers both normal and severe driving conditions
- Regular maintenance is critical for engine longevity and reliability
- Severe condition maintenance intervals are more frequent than normal conditions
- Special attention should be paid to oil changes and filter replacements under severe driving conditions
- The Iveco 8140.43P engine requires SAE 10W/40 oil with API SJ/CF standard
- Engine oil capacity: 7.3 liters (drained motor), 7.0 liters (including filter), 5.9 liters (without filters)
