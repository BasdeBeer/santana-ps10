# Parts Sourcing Preferences

## EU only

Source parts from within the EU only. No UK (post-Brexit customs and VAT hassle), no US, no AliExpress, no non-EU vendors. Shipping to the Netherlands.

Acceptable countries: NL, BE, DE, FR, IT, ES, AT, and other EU member states.

**Why:** Customs declarations, import VAT, and shipping delays from outside the EU make non-EU sourcing not worth the savings. Warranty claims on non-EU parts are also impractical.

**How to apply:** When researching parts, filter out UK / US / Asian vendors up front. Only present EU-based shops in recommendations. If a part is genuinely only available outside the EU, flag this explicitly so the owner can decide.
