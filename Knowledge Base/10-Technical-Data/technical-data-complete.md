## Performance Characteristics

### Engine Performance Summary

**Common Rail Variant (This PS10 - EDC Euro III):**
- Power: 88.8-95.2 kW (120.8-129.5 CV) at 3,600 rpm
- Torque: 275.5-304.7 Nm (28.1-31.1 kgm) at 1,800 rpm

**Mechanical Injection Variant (Reference):**
- Power: 75.5-80.5 kW (102.7-109.5 CV) at 3,600 rpm
- Torque: 250 Nm (25.5 kgm) at 1,800 rpm

*(Service Manual PS10, page 10-2)*

### Transmission Performance

**Gearbox:** ZF LT-85 5-speed manual with overdrive (0.795 ratio on 5th)
**Transfer:** Part-time 4WD with high (1.192) and low (3.320) ratios
**Final Drive:** 3.909 ratio (43:11 gearing)

*(Service Manual PS10, page 10-3; Owner's Manual, page 11-3)*

### Vehicle Dynamics

- **Turning Radius:** 7.3 m
- **Maximum Slope:** 45°
- **Fording Depth:** 500 mm
- **Ground Clearance:** 200 mm

*(Service Manual PS10, page 10-4)*
