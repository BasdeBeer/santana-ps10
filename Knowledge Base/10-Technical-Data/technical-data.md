## Key Performance Characteristics

### Engine Performance
- **Mechanical Injection variant:** 75.5-80.5 kW (102.7-109.5 CV) @ 3,600 rpm
- **Common Rail variant:** 88.8-95.2 kW (120.8-129.5 CV) @ 3,600 rpm
- **Peak torque:** 250 Nm (Mechanical) or 275.5-304.7 Nm (Common Rail) @ 1,800 rpm
- **Compression ratio:** 18.5:1 (high-compression diesel)

### Transmission Characteristics
- **5-speed manual gearbox** with overdrive (5th gear ratio: 0.795)
- **4WD transfer box** with high (1.192) and low (3.319) range selection
- **Final drive ratio:** 3.909 (43:11)

### Vehicle Dimensions and Capability
- **Wheelbase:** 2,786 mm
- **Turning radius:** 7.3 m
- **Maximum slope:** 45°
- **Fording depth:** 500 mm
- **Gross vehicle weight rating:** 3,050 kg (5-door configuration)
